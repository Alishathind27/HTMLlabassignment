Freezer Meals

When you want to freeze soup, allow it to cool completely before freezing it in airtight, freezer-safe containers. Transfer them to the fridge to thaw overnight and reheat the soup on the stove, or defrost frozen soup in the microwave.

Curried Lentil Salad

French green lentils – Unlike regular green or brown lentils, French green lentils hold their shape when cooked, and they have a nice al dente bite.

Paneer – Cubes of roasted paneer add a rich, savory, and chewy element to this salad. If you’re not familiar with paneer,it’s a fresh Indian cheese. Look for it in the cheese section of your grocery store.
